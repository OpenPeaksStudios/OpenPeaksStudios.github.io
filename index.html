Game Overview

Baker by Day

Run a bustling storefront bakery, serving customers, managing resources, and crafting a reputation worth protecting.

Fighter by Night

Operate an illegal underground mech arena after hours — baking living bread machines and piloting them to glory to promote your brand.

Every Recipe is a Decision

The same ingredients that make your bestselling loaf can forge a devastating combat golem. Balance your bakery's needs against your fighter's edge.

You run a bakery by day and an illegal underground mech arena by night — baking living bread machines that fight to promote your brand. Balance hands-on first-person mech combat with tactile, recipe-driven baking and business management.

How You Play

01

Recipe-Driven Combat

Collect ingredients and bake golems YOUR way, piloting them to victory. Ingredients unlock unique abilities that will put you in the upper crust!

02

Strategic Bakery Management

Balance counter space, customer orders, and golem crafting; all while fending off rogue pastry predators threatening your legacy.

03

Ingredient Affinity System

Every special ingredient you fold into your dough shapes how your golem fights. Experiment in the kitchen to craft a construct that moves and battles exactly the way you do.
